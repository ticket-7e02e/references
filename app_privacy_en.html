2. TERMS AND DEFINITIONS

2.1. Application – the mobile application "Ticket Check", designed for reading and analyzing transport cards, as well as providing Users with information about travel tickets, available for download and installation on mobile devices running the Android operating system.

2.2. User – any individual who has installed the Application on a mobile device and/or uses it.

2.3. Transport Card – a contactless smart card used for travel on public transport, including "Troika", "Strelka", "Podorozhnik" cards, social cards, bank cards with a transport application, as well as other media containing information about travel tickets, balance, and trips.

2.4. Card Data – information read from the transport card when it is brought to the User's mobile device, including: unique card identifier (UID); card type and its technical characteristics; contents of memory sectors (bitmap); information about recorded tickets and passes; data on balance and number of trips; card usage history; other information recorded on the card by the issuer.

2.5. Data Processing – any action (operation) or set of actions (operations) performed with or without the use of automation tools on information, including collection, recording, systematization, accumulation, storage, clarification (updating, modification), extraction, use, transfer (distribution, provision, access), depersonalization, blocking, deletion, destruction of information.

3. WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT

3.1. Information obtained when reading transport cards:

3.1.1. When a transport card is brought to the User's mobile device, the Application reads the following data: card UID (Unique Identifier); card type (MIFARE Classic, MIFARE Ultralight, etc.); contents of available memory sectors (bitmap); information about travel tickets (ticket type, validity period, number of trips); electronic wallet balance data (if available); information about recent transactions (date, time, place of use); issuer code and card issuance region; identifiers of transport applications recorded on the card; information about benefits and passenger category (for social cards); other information provided by the card format.

3.1.2. The read information is used solely to display it to the User in a comprehensible form and to store the check history on the User's device.

3.2. Information about the User's device:

3.2.1. To ensure the Application's functionality and collect statistics, we automatically receive the following information: model and manufacturer of the mobile device; Android operating system version; Application version; unique device identifier (anonymized Android ID); presence and version of the NFC module; language settings and device region; time zone; network connection information (network type).

3.3. Information about the use of the Application:

3.3.1. In order to improve the quality of the Application and analyze user experience, we collect the following information: number of cards read; types of cards detected; statistics on the use of Application functions; information about errors and crashes (crash reports); time and date of Application use; session duration.

3.4. Information voluntarily provided by the User:

3.4.1. When contacting support or providing feedback, the User may provide: email address; username; text of the message; screenshots (if necessary).

3.4.2. This information is used solely to respond to the inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

3.5. Information that is NOT collected by the Application:

3.5.1. The Application does NOT collect or process the following categories of information: personal data allowing direct identification of an individual (full name, residential address, passport details), except when such information is part of the social card data and is read from it; geographic coordinates and location of the User; contacts and phone book; photos and videos from the device gallery; data from other applications; passwords and access keys; payment information and bank card data; browser history and bookmarks; SMS messages and call logs; biometric data.

4. PURPOSES OF INFORMATION COLLECTION AND PROCESSING

4.1. Main purposes of information processing:

4.1.1. Ensuring the functioning of the Application: reading and decoding information from transport cards; displaying the read information to the User in a clear and structured form; storing the check history on the User's device; providing the ability to search and filter the saved history.

4.1.2. Improving the quality of the Application: analyzing card types to add support for new formats; identifying and fixing errors and crashes; optimizing performance; analyzing the popularity of various functions; collecting usage statistics.

4.1.3. Technical support: handling User inquiries; advising on issues related to the Application's operation; assisting in solving problems.
